Rilakkuma-san

Rilakkuma

Rilakkuma's name is a combination of リラックス rirakkusu, a Japanese transliteration of the English word "relax", and クマ（熊）kuma, the Japanese word for "bear".

Origin

He mysteriously appears in the office lady Kaoru's apartment one day.[9] He is a soft toy bear who has apparently decided to take up residence there.
